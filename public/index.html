About Me

I was born and raised right here in the beutiful city of Seattle. After completing high school, I attended Triton College and St. Xavier University in Chicago Illinois.In order to pay off student loans I accrued in college, I decided to enlist in the U.S. Army to take advantage of the Student Loan Repayment program. After four and half years, and two deployments to the Middle East I was honorably discharged back into the civilian world.

Once back in civilian life, I struggled to find that "thing" that I could be passionate about. It took a couple years and trying and failing at multiple things, but I finally found coding and haven't looked back since.
